Practical4-Digitizing Features

This exercise is very interesting and requires us to analyze it according to our own design and data.

I have made a lot of attempts and made a lot of changes, I hope I can use different methods as much as possible while successfully completing the task.

  • 1. I create a buffer of light of lamps.
  • 2. The second analysis is much more difficult. I want to get the roads that do not cover by light of lamps. I got the result after several steps.
  • 3. Then, create a buffer to verify if there is a car park within 500meters away from each house.
  • 4. And I choose one of the house as an example to show how to find the nearest car park
  • 5. At last, I use statistic tools to do some analysis
