Privacy Policy for MaaS-MTR

Last Updated: February 2026

MaaS-MTR ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using MaaS-MTR, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

MaaS-MTR may collect the following types of data to provide its core travel planning and research functions:

1.1 User Account Information

When you create an account, we generate a random user identifier (username) for service purposes.

  • User ID (randomly generated)
  • No email address or phone number is required

1.2 Travel Schedule and User Content

The App allows users to record travel-related schedules and activity plans, including:

  • Daily travel plans (weekday/weekend)
  • Activity types and timing
  • Selected transport modes and preferences
  • Questionnaire responses related to travel behavior

This information is treated as user-provided content.

1.3 Location Information

To support travel planning and mobility analysis, the App may collect location-related information, such as:

  • Activity locations entered by the user
  • Trip origin/destination points
  • Cross-border travel indicators (e.g., Hong Kong‚ÄìShenzhen)

Location data is collected only for providing App functionality and research purposes.

1.4 Usage Data (Optional)

We may collect limited interaction data to improve user experience, such as:

  • Selected travel recommendations
  • Feature usage patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information only for the following purposes:

  • Providing core MaaS-MTR services (travel planning, schedule recording)
  • Supporting integrated mobility recommendations
  • Conducting academic research on travel behavior adaptation under MaaS systems
  • Improving App performance, functionality, and user experience

We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your data will only be disclosed if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users and the platform.

4. Data Storage and Security

All collected data is stored securely on our servers.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide App services and support research objectives.

When data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted.

You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
